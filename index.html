Real-time monitoring. Uncomplicated visualization.

Monitor your buses and find out where they are without the need for manual communication. Understand your driver's performance as well as incidents and events that could affect your commuters.

Making the drivers part of the solution

The PASADA mobile app tracks not only the location using GPS technology but also the vehicle's speed and informs the driver if the maximum speed has been reached. Access to emergency services for life-threatening situations and vehicle breakdowns are just a tap-away.
